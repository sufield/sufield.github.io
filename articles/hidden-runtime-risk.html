The Hidden Runtime Risk Nobody Tracks

Problem:
Modern systems change shape during runtime. Traffic shifts, retries spike, caches stall, dependencies slow down. These shifts create unsafe states that no deployment tool or static config change can see.

Runtime Risk Is Invisible Until It Explodes

Why It Matters:
Failures start long before an outage. Small drifts accumulate until the system crosses a boundary that no human is watching. These conditions are invisible in dashboards and logs.

Impact:
Teams treat incidents as surprises because the system had no mechanism to declare, "I am no longer safe."
