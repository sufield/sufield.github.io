Observability Alone Cannot Prevent Outages

Problem:
Observability shows what already happened. It does not enforce guardrails. Metrics and traces cannot block an unsafe action.

Observability Watches Problems Happen

Why It Matters:
During an incident, dashboards only confirm the damage. They do not stop it. Engineers rely on alerts and manual actions while the system keeps drifting.

Impact:
Organizations become reactive. They see issues but cannot act fast enough.
