About

Modern cloud systems fail for one reason: they drift into unsafe states faster than humans or tools can react. This site introduces a new engineering approach—the Runtime Control Plane (RCP)—that applies proven principles from control systems, safety engineering, and distributed systems to keep cloud environments within safe boundaries at all times. The goal is simple: prevent outages before they begin, reduce recovery to seconds, and give engineers a clearer model of how their systems behave. Want to see how RCP solves this?
